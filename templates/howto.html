    Back Office

    Editorial chiefs/directors could see in real time the most discussed news among their newspapers and/or their competitors.

    End Users

    Mozaic can be used by the readers to browse around the most important news. Even when mozaic pulls the information from several different newspapers, it can direct the traffic to your website to find that particular the news article the reader is interested on.
